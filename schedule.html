Team Race Schedule

Clash of the Inland Northwest

3rd, 103 points:
This meet was a great start to the season. This race went a little faster than it has in previous years, and our men went even faster. This was also only a 6 kilometer race.

Courger Classic

10th, 211 points:
This meet was another chance for our men to show off. We had some fast times for an 8 kilometer race and we faced lots of big teams in the NCAA D1 West region.

Gans Creek Invitational

9th, 219 points:
This was a defining meet for our men. We raced against the best teams in the NAIA in the midwest and did great. Some men were sick and others stepped up. We were ranked 9th in the nation after this meet.

Willamette Invitational

19th, 536 points:
This was a partial squad, the runners who did not race in Missouri. Solid racing for our 11th-16th men on the team.

LC Invite

5th, 108 points:
A decent showing for the LC men, and an great showing for the women. The men had six runners under 26:00, but the top five average was 25:05, the fastest this season. The men are looking strong with plenty of depth, something that will be a great strength when we race ten men for conference in three weeks.

Cascade Conference Championship
